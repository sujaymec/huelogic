One on One Mentoring

Every kid deserves a unique mentoring as they possess a unique set of skills, strengths and weakness. In iDC, our experienced mentors understand the need and practice the skills to nurture and guide every kid to perform their best. Our counselors have walked successfully in different career paths like Medicine, Engineering, Law, Architecture, and many more. They are alumni of renowned colleges like IIT's, IIM's, NID, NIFT, NLU, SRCC, LSR, St. Stephens, IIM and National school of Drama. We have walked the path and now we are there to support every student individually to reach the destination.

One essential C at a right time can answer the 4 big Cs; Career, Course, Country and College. Wondering what the essential C is? It stands for counseling. With One on One Mentoring, we help students in making well informed decisions in every step of their life.


  • We help students understand their Skills, Strengths and Weaknesses
  • Based on this assessment we help them select career best suited for them
  • We also guide them identify best collages and country options for the selected course

We understand the role of parents in shaping the kid's career and future. Hence, we act as an end to end support structure for the parents and kids at every imperative step.
