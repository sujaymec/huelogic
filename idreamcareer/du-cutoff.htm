Why is it Important

  • Delhi University is one of the pioneering universities in India.
  • During the admission time, there is a lot of ambiguity regarding the DU cut-offs.
  • The cut-off scores are very dynamic and keeps changing every year.

Common Issues with Cut-offs

  • It's difficult to plan future course of action due to the dynamic cut-offs of DU
  • Many students are left disheartened every year when they find out that they don't qualify due to the high cut-off
  • Some students don't apply expecting a high cut off and later realize that they could have got a seat.
  • Students find themselves un-prepared when the cut-offs vary from their expectations.
  • Admission in many other universities are closed by the time the Delhi cut offs are released.

DU Cut-off Prediction Tool

  • iDC has devised a prediction tool that can predict the cut-off scores for Delhi University courses and colleges.
  • After compiling the cut off scores of previous years, we have identified a trend which enables us to make an educated prediction.
  • Cut off prediction tool helps the students in getting a guesstimate of the probable cut off for various Delhi University courses.
  • Student won't have to struggle with long spreadsheets of cut off scores of past years.
