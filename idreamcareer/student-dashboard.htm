Student Dashboard

iDC has designed a systematic way to keep track of every step in student's career selection progress. We provide students with a dashboard that holds all vital information regarding various career options. The dashboard displays studentâ€™s psychometric analysis report, which is analytically divided to focus on three important factors: Aptitude, Personality and Interest. We further help student with 'Career Fitment Analysis', which provides students with a graph on how they fit the requirements of various career options.

The dashboard helps in creating an academic profile for every student which has their academic and personal details. It also equips them with tools to gain verified and well researched information about colleges and careers. Following are some key tabs which a student can use in our dashboard:


  • Assessment Centre: This tab holds many vital assessments that help the student analyze themselves and decide a career accordingly. Here, the student can choose from a number of tests to gain insights about their interests, personality and aptitude.
  • Mentoring Centre: Once students take the 'One on One Mentoring' services, individual counselors are allotted to each student. These mentors provide comprehensive support to students. Here they can also schedule a session with their mentors and ask queries related to career and studies.
  • Careers Exploration: This section offers student an extensive list of more than 250 career pathways. This tab also provides the student with some most suitable career options, based on the student's psychometric assessment
  • College Exploration: Career exploration provides well researched information on all reputed colleges in India through a strategically designed tool. This tool filters the colleges on various parameters like location, fees, affiliation, courses offered, etc.
