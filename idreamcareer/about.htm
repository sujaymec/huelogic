What we do?

iDreamCareer.com (iDC) is India's leading education company in the space of career planning for students. With the growing problem for students in deciding a career for themselves, iDC developed a 3S framework program - "Structured, Scientific & Scalable", which is currently being used by over 1, 00, 000 (1 lac) students every year in India, Middle East & East Africa. iDC has been known for its innovation in this space by launching the first tangible physical career planning product - "iDC Career Planning Kit" (career counseling in a box).

iDC Career Guidance Program empower students to take informed career decisions via iDC Career Assessment tool, access to information and one-on-one career mentoring.

iDC boast of its largest board of quality mentors (220 as on date) from various professions, colleges and psychology background who have helped over 1.5 lac students in the last 2 years.

iDC Career assessment tool which is available in English, Hindi, Marathi and Kannada language has been used by 47,000 high school students till date and 21 renowned career counsellors prescribe our assessment tool to high school students.

iDC currently have a team of 15 full time people with offices in New Delhi (South Extension & Rohini), Navi Mumbai, Bangalore, Kanpur, Hyderabad.

iDC was selected as the top 14 fastest growing companies in India by CNBC TV 18 in its reality show "Masterpreneurs Award 2014". It was selected as the Best Social Enterprise by IIM, Indore in 2012 and selected as one of the 6 enterprise in SEED Program at Villgro.
