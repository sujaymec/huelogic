Benefits for Students and Parents

iDC aims to provide an comprehensive service which is helpful for both thee students and their parents. iDC counselors understand that it is difficult for a student to figure out what is best for him especially when he is subjected to peer pressure. It is very common for a student to get allured by a popular career choice rather than a suitable career choice and follow the herd mentality. On the other hand, parents also sometimes find it difficult to understand the child's interest and fail to guide him effectively. iDC counselors with their expertise in student psychology can help students in finding their most suitable careers and also help parents in connecting their thoughts with the child Following are some benefits of associating with iDC:


Benefits to Student:

  • We encourage self-discovery, so students can find the career which is most suitable for his skills.
  • iDC provides individual mentors to student for one on one mentoring. Students also get a personalized online mentor to guide them throughout the year, irrespective of location.
  • Students learn more about their personality traits, skills and interests. They explore the right career options based on these traits.
  • iDC provides the students a platform where they can filter and search for colleges and apply for scholarships accordingly.

Benefits to Parent:

  • iDC helps the parents in understanding their ward's aptitude and talent through specially designed psychometric tests.
  • The scientific analysis enables the parents to help the child in taking the right career decisions and avoid social influences.
  • iDC helps parents in gaining a neutral perspective on their children's career selection process.
  • Parents gain accurate information about a large number of career options.
  • Parents get a clear idea of cost implications for preparation and education in selected career choice
