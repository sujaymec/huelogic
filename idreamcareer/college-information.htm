College Information

Selecting the right college is one of the most crucial ingredients of a successful career. Although, there is a lot of information available on the college websites, it is still a laborious task to refine the factual information from propaganda. Moreover, there are a large number of colleges available in all streams which make finding the right colleges more exhausting. Most of the time the college search process doesn't complete even after finding a top ranking college, one has to also scan the colleges on various parameters. This process is very laborious and time taking.

iDC 'explore college' feature makes it easy for the students and parents to find the best suited college. iDC has assembled enormous information regarding a large list of colleges. The easy to use, 'explore college' option makes it very easy to search colleges and to gain knowledge regarding it. The colleges have also been sorted on the basis of various parameters like location, fees, affiliation, courses offered, etc. This allows the students to either simply look into the profiles of the colleges in India or to go further and search colleges based on one or more parameters. This feature is very helpful in restricting the search to the colleges which fits your expectations and eliminate the rest and hence reduce confusion.
