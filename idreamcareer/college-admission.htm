College Admission

We can help you find the top colleges and best courses. Our team of experts will provide an end to end support for college admission and course
What after 12th? Is this question ringing in your head all the time? Confused with which courses or colleges to choose? You are not the only one!

Common Dilemmas:

  • Offline Admission: Many of the colleges in India does not have online admissions, how to research and find out their admission procedures?
  • Too Many Options: Internet is filled with colleges, each one promising to be the best for the kid.
  • Parameters: What are the things we should consider while selecting a college?
  • Confusions: With so many options and limited time in hand, how to make sure we choose the college best suited for the kid?
  • Placement: How to find out the past placement records of the colleges?
  • City: We know the colleges in our city, but how to find out good colleges in other cities to apply?
  • Shortlisting: How to create the list of colleges and universities right for the kid, where we need to apply?
  • Forms and Application Deadlines: Where to get the application forms? How to make sure we apply in time and don't miss a single deadline?
  • Queries: It's hard to reach the colleges with our queries about them. Is there a way we can get in touch with someone who can answer our queries?

How can iDC help:

  • Personalized Counselling: We believe that every kid is unique and deserves unique career guidance. Hence, the student will receive personalised college counselling through telephone or Skype.
  • Assistance in application forms Procurement & filling: We understand that application process can be a tedious and confusing task. We will provide an end to end application support in filling the admission forms
  • Forms & Exam Deadline Alert via email: It's natural to forget few deadlines when you have a lot going in your mind. iDC will remind you of every important milestone regarding form filling and exam deadlines.
  • Email Support: 'Ask and thou shall receive', queries are always good in life. iDC provides an extensive email support to resolve student's queries.

How can iDC help:

  • End to end college admission guidance
  • College shortlisting with extensive college comparison pan India
  • Information on colleges and universities pan India
  • Systematic approach to guide you in selecting and finalizing colleges
  • Support from experienced and dedicated counsellors to help you finalize colleges after 12th science, arts, commerce, etc.
  • Assistance with procuring and filing of college forms
  • College application help with wide spread colleges across India, like colleges in Delhi, Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore, etc.
  • Best for those students who have just appeared for their 12th grade exam and need some quick college admission guidance for moving further.
