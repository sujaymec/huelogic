About

Pravesh is the co-founder of iDreamCareer.com. He is an alumnus of IIT, Kharagpur. After completing his B.Tech & M.Tech in Electronics from IIT, he co-founded GreenHat Ventures (An IIT Kharagpur incubated company) in the field of assessments and employability training services for colleges. Pravesh worked with Educomp Solutions Ltd. as product head for K12 assessment space catering to more than 500 schools and later worked with IndiaCan Education Pvt. Ltd. as strategy consultant in the space of vocational education and distance education segment. Pravesh has attended Tata Jagriti Yatra and actively takes part in social causes and initiatives. Pravesh was selected as one of the 14 entrepreneurs in India by CNBC TV 18 for its award show Masterpreneurs. Pravesh is passionate about the huge potential of our young generation and plays an active role in youth leadership by helping them make better careers for themselves.
