iDC's Career Assessment

Every year, the winter wind blows in a big question that haunts students - what to do next? This is the time when students decide on their college and career. This is a stressful time not only for the student but also for the parents. How can we guide the students and parents in taking an informed decision? iDC has the best solution to this problem: iDC Career Assessment.

What is iDC Career Assessment?

iDC, along with a team of expert psychologists, has developed a first of its kind career assessment tool. Unlike other career assessment tools, this tool has the advantage of being designed keeping in mind the Indian student's profile. It evaluates the students on three vital factors - Aptitude, Personality, and Interest. Moreover, this test is efficiently designed in 3 sections which can be comfortably completed by the student in 90 minutes.

iDC Career Assessment has been taken by 26,000 students within the last 2 years

iDC Career Assessment has achieved the required validity and reliability scores as below: The reliability of the Aptitude Test - calculated using Cronbach Alpha is (a=0.64) The reliability of the Personality Test - calculated using Cronbach Alpha is (a=0.79) The reliability of the Interest Test - calculated using Cronbach Alpha is (a=0.78)



Why Should a Student opt for iDC Career Assessment?

With the multitude of career options, it is very natural for the students to feel perplexed in deciding the ones that are most suitable for them. iDC Career Assessment helps in the following ways:

  • iDC creates a comprehensive 17 page career report after the assessment.
  • This consists of a detailed analysis of the student's interest, aptitude and personality.
  • It assesses the student's career fitment among 21 career clusters.
  • iDC also goes an extra step by detailing the stream fitment analysis which is instrumental in helping the 9th and 10th grade students in subject selection.
  • iDC has a team of qualified career counselors who have expertise in guiding the students to identify the career best for them and in dealing with the requirements of that particular career.
  • The specialized career assessment tests conducted by the iDC counselors provide a comprehensive support to evaluate the student's skills and to spot the weaknesses so that an efficient teaching platform can be created for the student.
  • iDC has a pan India presence and have successfully helped many students and parents from every part of India.
  • Respecting the diversity in India, iDC Career Assessment Test and its report is made available in 5 languages - English, Hindi, Assamese, Kannada and Marathi.
